This is a panel about getting involved with your local game development community! We have a stacked panel of community leaders from all over (Boston, NYC, and Seattle) hosted and moderated by Gavin Camlin, an organizer from the local Boston area! Join us as we delve deep into different questions like "How do I find a local game development community?", "What are the values of going to local events?", "How do I get involved with organizing events?".

Whether you're a professional, hobbyist, or just an avid gamer, game development communities have something interesting for everyone!
